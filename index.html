Introduction

Welcome to SampleKing.

SampleKing is a music discovery and sample exploration application that helps users identify music and discover the samples, artists, records, and cultural connections behind the songs they love.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you regarding your information.

By using SampleKing, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Music Recognition Data

SampleKing uses your device's microphone to identify music playing nearby.

When you initiate music recognition:

  • Audio is temporarily processed for music identification purposes.
  • Audio is not recorded, stored, sold, or used for advertising purposes.
  • Audio is used solely to identify the music currently playing and provide related information within the app.

SampleKing does not create a library of microphone recordings.

Usage and Analytics Data

We collect information about how users interact with SampleKing in order to improve the app and understand feature usage.

Examples may include:

  • App launches
  • Recognition attempts
  • Recognition success and failure rates
  • Sample discovery activity
  • Feature engagement
  • Navigation behavior
  • History usage
  • Outbound link interactions
  • Session duration
  • Performance metrics
  • Error and diagnostic information

This information is used exclusively for:

  • Product improvement
  • Feature development
  • Reliability monitoring
  • Performance optimization
  • User experience analysis

We do not use analytics data for advertising or cross-app tracking.

Session Replay Data

SampleKing may use session replay technology to help improve usability and diagnose issues.

Session replay may capture:

  • Screen navigation
  • Taps
  • Scrolling behavior
  • Interface interactions
  • Screen transitions

Session replay is used solely for product improvement, bug investigation, performance analysis, and user experience optimization.

Session replay data is not used for advertising, profiling, or cross-app tracking.

Device and Diagnostic Information

We may collect limited technical information about your device and application usage, including:

  • App version
  • Operating system version
  • Device type
  • Session information
  • Error events
  • Performance metrics

Recent Listen History

SampleKing may store a limited history of recently identified tracks directly on your device to improve your experience.

This history may include:

  • Detected track information
  • Associated sample information
  • Artwork and metadata
  • Timestamps related to recognition activity

Affiliate Links and Referral Programs

SampleKing may contain links to third-party services, music platforms, ticketing providers, merchandise providers, record marketplaces, and other music-related services.

Some of these links may be affiliate links.

If you click an affiliate link and complete a qualifying action, SampleKing may receive a commission or referral fee at no additional cost to you.

Affiliate interactions are used to measure feature effectiveness, evaluate user engagement, and support development of the application.

Affiliate interactions are not used for advertising profiles, behavioral targeting, or cross-app tracking.

Third-Party Services

SampleKing may integrate with third-party services including:

  • Mixpanel
  • Apple Music
  • Spotify
  • Discogs
  • MusicBrainz
  • Cover Art Archive
  • Ticketing providers
  • Merchandise providers
  • Affiliate partners

Tracking and Advertising

SampleKing is not designed as an advertising platform.

We do not use collected information to:

  • Build advertising profiles
  • Deliver targeted advertising
  • Retarget users across applications or websites
  • Share user data with advertising networks

Children's Privacy

SampleKing is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
