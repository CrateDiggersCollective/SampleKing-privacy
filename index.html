# SampleKing Privacy Policy **Last Updated:** June 2026 ## Introduction Welcome to SampleKing. SampleKing is a music discovery and sample exploration application that helps users identify music and discover the samples, artists, records, and cultural connections behind the songs they love. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you regarding your information. By using SampleKing, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. --- # Information We Collect ## Music Recognition Data SampleKing uses your device's microphone to identify music playing nearby. When you initiate music recognition: * Audio is temporarily processed for music identification purposes. * Audio is not recorded, stored, sold, or used for advertising purposes. * Audio is used solely to identify the music currently playing and provide related information within the app. SampleKing does not create a library of microphone recordings. --- ## Usage and Analytics Data We collect information about how users interact with SampleKing in order to improve the app and understand feature usage. Examples may include: * App launches * Recognition attempts * Recognition success and failure rates * Sample discovery activity * Feature engagement * Navigation behavior * History usage * Outbound link interactions * Session duration * Performance metrics * Error and diagnostic information This information is used exclusively for: * Product improvement * Feature development * Reliability monitoring * Performance optimization * User experience analysis We do not use analytics data for advertising or cross-app tracking. --- ## Session Replay Data SampleKing may use session replay technology to help improve usability and diagnose issues. Session replay may capture: * Screen navigation * Taps * Scrolling behavior * Interface interactions * Screen transitions Session replay is used solely for: * Product improvement * Bug investigation * Performance analysis * User experience optimization Session replay data is not used for advertising, profiling, or cross-app tracking. Where applicable, sensitive information and protected fields are masked or excluded from recording. --- ## Device and Diagnostic Information We may collect limited technical information about your device and application usage, including: * App version * Operating system version * Device type * Session information * Error events * Performance metrics This information helps us maintain and improve SampleKing. --- # Recent Listen History SampleKing may store a limited history of recently identified tracks directly on your device to improve your experience. This history may include: * Detected track information * Associated sample information * Artwork and metadata * Timestamps related to recognition activity Recent listen history is used solely to provide app functionality and user convenience. --- # Affiliate Links and Referral Programs SampleKing may contain links to third-party services, music platforms, ticketing providers, merchandise providers, record marketplaces, and other music-related services. Affiliate interactions are used to: * Measure feature effectiveness * Evaluate user engagement * Support development of the application Affiliate interactions are not used for advertising profiles, behavioral targeting, or cross-app tracking. --- # Third-Party Services SampleKing may integrate with third-party services to provide functionality, analytics, metadata, music information, and related content. Examples may include: * Mixpanel * Apple Music * Spotify * Discogs * MusicBrainz * Cover Art Archive * Ticketing providers * Merchandise providers * Affiliate partners Each third-party service operates under its own privacy practices and policies. We encourage users to review the privacy policies of any third-party services they choose to use. --- # How We Use Information We use collected information to: * Operate SampleKing * Identify music * Display sample relationships * Improve application performance * Diagnose issues * Measure feature usage * Understand engagement trends * Support affiliate partnerships * Enhance the user experience We do not sell personal information. --- # Tracking and Advertising SampleKing is not designed as an advertising platform.
